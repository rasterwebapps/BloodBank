# Quick Reference — Donor Portal

**Role**: Donor
**Last Updated**: 2026-04-21
**Portal**: Donor Portal → `https://bloodbank.example.com/donor`

> This guide helps you use the BloodBank Donor Portal to manage your donations, appointments, and health records.

---

## Creating Your Account

If you are a new donor, a staff member at the blood bank will register you and send you an invitation email.

1. Open the invitation email from bloodbank@example.com
2. Click **Activate My Account**
3. Set a password (minimum 12 characters, one uppercase, one number, one symbol)
4. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (recommended): scan the QR code with an authenticator app
5. Click **Get Started**

---

## Logging In

1. Go to `https://bloodbank.example.com/donor`
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Enter your **email** and **password**
4. Enter the 2FA code if enabled

**Forgot password?** Click _Forgot password_ on the login page and follow the email link.

---

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard shows:

- **Eligibility status**: whether you are currently eligible to donate
- **Next eligible date**: if you are in a cooling-off period
- **Upcoming appointment**: date, time, and branch
- **Donation history**: summary of all your past donations
- **Your blood group**: confirmed after your first donation

---

## Booking an Appointment

**Menu**: Appointments → Book Appointment

1. Select a blood bank branch near you
2. Choose an available date from the calendar
3. Select a time slot
4. Click **Confirm Booking**
5. You will receive an SMS and/or email confirmation

> If no slots are shown, the branch has no availability on that date. Try a different date or branch.

### Rescheduling or Cancelling

**Menu**: Appointments → My Appointments

1. Click the appointment
2. Click **Reschedule** or **Cancel**
3. If rescheduling: select a new date and time
4. Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance so your slot can be offered to others

---

## Viewing Your Donation History

**Menu**: History → My Donations

Each donation record shows:
- Date and branch
- Component donated (Whole Blood, Platelets, etc.)
- Volume (mL)
- Collection outcome (Successful / Adverse Reaction)

---

## Viewing Your Health Screening Results

**Menu**: Health → Screening Results

After each donation, your test results are available here once the Lab has processed them:
- HIV: Reactive / Non-Reactive
- Hepatitis B: Reactive / Non-Reactive
- Hepatitis C: Reactive / Non-Reactive
- Syphilis: Reactive / Non-Reactive

> If any result is **Reactive**, the blood bank will contact you directly with a referral for confirmatory testing. Your donation was not used.

---

## Checking Your Eligibility

**Menu**: Health → Eligibility Check

The system shows your current eligibility based on:
- Time since last donation (56 days for whole blood, 14 days for platelets)
- Any temporary deferral (illness, medication, travel)
- Any permanent deferral

If you believe your eligibility status is incorrect, contact the blood bank.

---

## Updating Your Contact Details

**Menu**: Profile → Edit Profile

You can update:
- Phone number
- Email address
- Postal address
- Emergency contact
- Notification preferences (SMS / Email / Both)

> Blood group and date of birth cannot be self-edited. Contact the blood bank to correct these.

---

## Downloading Your Donor Card

**Menu**: Profile → Donor Card

1. Click **Download Donor Card** (PDF)
2. You can print it or save it to your phone

---

## Managing Your Consent Preferences

**Menu**: Profile → Consent & Privacy

You can manage:
- Consent to be contacted for specific blood group needs (emergency campaigns)
- Research participation consent
- Data retention preferences

> Withdrawing consent does not delete your historical donation records, which are required by law for traceability.

---

## Requesting Your Data (GDPR)

**Menu**: Profile → Privacy → Download My Data

1. Click **Request Data Export**
2. The system will email you a downloadable ZIP file within 72 hours

To request deletion of your personal data, contact the blood bank at privacy@bloodbank.example.com. Note that donation traceability records may be retained as legally required.

---

## Getting Help

| Issue | Action |
|---|---|
| Cannot log in | Click _Forgot password_ on the login page |
| Appointment not showing | Check your email confirmation; contact branch if missing |
| Eligibility status seems wrong | Contact the blood bank at the branch where you donated |
| Data question | Email privacy@bloodbank.example.com |
| Technical issue | Email support@bloodbank.example.com |
